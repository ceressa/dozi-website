Dozi vs Medisafe: Which Is Better? 2026 Comparison

A detailed comparison of features, pricing, privacy, and family tracking between two leading medication apps.

Choosing the right medication reminder app is a decision that directly affects your health outcomes. Dozi and Medisafe are two of the most popular options in this space. But which one is the better fit for your needs? In this guide, we compare both apps across eight key criteria.

Dozi and Medisafe: A Quick Overview

Dozi is a medication tracking app developed in Turkey. It stands out with its time-based and location-based reminders, family tracking system (Badi), medication stock management, and privacy-first design. Its interface is fully localized for Turkish users while also supporting English.

Medisafe is a US-based medication reminder widely used worldwide. It offers a large drug database, drug interaction checking, and support for both Android and iOS. However, several core features require a premium subscription.

3 Areas Where Dozi Excels

1. Badi Family Tracking System

Dozi's Badi system lets family members track medication adherence without creating a sense of surveillance. You can see whether an elderly parent has taken their medication without resorting to the tension-inducing "Did you take your pill?" calls. While Medisafe offers a similar concept with Medfriend, it is neither as seamless nor as free as Badi.

2. Location-Based Reminders

Dozi can send reminders not only at specific times but also when you arrive at specific locations. For example, you can receive a notification when you get home or reach your office. This feature makes a significant difference for people with irregular work schedules. Medisafe does not offer this capability.

3. Privacy and Data Security

Dozi collects minimal personal data and is fully GDPR compliant. Your health data is never used for advertising purposes. Medisafe, on the other hand, operates a business model that includes data-sharing agreements with pharmaceutical companies. If data privacy is a priority for you, this is a meaningful distinction.

Areas Where Medisafe Excels

Drug Interaction Checking

Medisafe has an extensive drug database and checks for potential interactions if you take multiple medications. This feature is particularly valuable for patients on complex medication regimens who want preliminary information before consulting their doctor.

iOS Support

If you are an iPhone user, Medisafe works on both platforms. Dozi is currently available only on Android. If someone in your family uses an iPhone, this could influence your platform choice.

Verdict: Finding the Right Fit

Both apps are reliable tools for medication tracking. However, your decision should be guided by your specific needs.

If you value privacy, want a free and seamless family tracking experience, need location-based reminders, or prefer a clean minimalist interface, Dozi is the better choice for you.

If you use an iPhone and drug interaction checking is critical for your regimen, Medisafe is also a solid option. However, keep in mind the premium subscription costs and broader data collection practices.
