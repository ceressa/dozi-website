ℹ️ General

What is Dozi?
Dozi is an AI-powered medication tracking and reminder app. It helps improve your medication adherence with smart reminders, family tracking (Badi system), and stock tracking features. Our goal is to simplify medication management and support your treatment journey.
Is Dozi free?
Yes, Dozi's core features are completely free. You can use medication reminders, stock tracking, and basic Badi features at no cost. For advanced features, you can opt for the Dozi Extra subscription.
What devices does Dozi work on?
Dozi is currently available on Android devices. You can download it for free from the Google Play Store. Development for the iOS version is underway and will be released soon.
How can I download Dozi?
You can download Dozi completely free from the Google Play Store. Search for "Dozi" or click here to go directly to the download page.

💊 Medication Tracking

Can I add multiple medications?
Yes, you can add unlimited medications to Dozi. You can set separate reminder times, dosage information, and stock tracking for each medication. Create reminders on daily, weekly, or custom schedules.
How does medication stock tracking work?
Enter your current tablet count when adding a medication. Dozi automatically counts down with each dose taken and alerts you when your stock is running low. This way, you can visit the pharmacy before running out and avoid interruptions in your treatment.
What is location-based reminding?
Location-based reminding sends you a medication reminder when you arrive at a specific location (such as home or work). Instead of a fixed time, your location acts as the trigger. It's especially ideal for people with irregular schedules or those who travel frequently.
What happens if I miss a notification?
Dozi will remind you again. With its escalation system, it sends persistent but gentle reminders to ensure you take your medication. When you miss the first reminder, you'll receive follow-up notifications at set intervals.

👪 Family & Badi

What is the Badi system?
The Badi system is a feature that allows family members to remotely track their loved ones' medication adherence. Badis cannot see medication names — they can only track whether medications were taken or not. This way, privacy is maintained while family peace of mind is ensured.
How do I get added as a Badi?
You can be added as a Badi using an invitation code generated within the app. You use the same Dozi app to track your loved ones' medication adherence. The person sharing the invitation code adds you as a Badi.
Can there be multiple Badis?
Yes, multiple people can be added as Badis. Siblings and spouses can track their family elders' medication together. Everyone can see the current status at the same time and provide support collectively.

🔒 Security & Privacy

Is my data safe?
Yes, Dozi is KVKK and GDPR compliant. All health data is stored encrypted and protected with the highest security standards. Your data security is our top priority.
Do you share my data with third parties?
No, never. Your data belongs only to you and is never shared with any third party. We do not sell or share data for advertising purposes.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can request account deletion from within the app or from the website. When your account is deleted, all your data is permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

⚙️ Technical

Is an internet connection required?
Reminders work offline — no internet connection is needed. However, Badi synchronization and some advanced features require an internet connection. Core medication reminders work in all conditions.
